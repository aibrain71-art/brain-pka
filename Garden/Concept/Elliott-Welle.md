The Elliott-Welle (Elliott Wave) is a technical analysis concept used to forecast market trends in financial markets. Developed by Ralph Nelson Elliott in 1938, it is based on fractal patterns, the Fibonacci sequence, and the golden ratio, reflecting collective investor psychology.
