Anson Belt (also known as Anson Belt & Buckle) is a family‑owned American company founded in 2009 by Frank Ferree and his son David Ferree. The brand sells micro‑adjustable, hole‑less belts with interchangeable buckles and straps, marketed as a perfect‑fit, lifetime‑guarantee product.
