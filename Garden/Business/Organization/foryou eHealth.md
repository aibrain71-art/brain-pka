foryou eHealth GmbH offers home usage self-tests and nutritional supplements to help individuals understand their blood values and optimize their health. Their products include self-tests for essential amino acid analysis, which provide detailed insights into blood values and offer personalized recommendations for nutritional supplements. The company emphasizes the use of high-quality, bioavailable ingredients and minimizes the use of additives and heavy metals in their products.
