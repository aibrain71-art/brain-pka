Cody Sanchez is an American entrepreneur, investor, and business strategist known for founding Contrarian Thinking, a financial media platform with millions of followers, and co‑founding Unconventional Acquisitions, which teaches people how to buy and run small businesses. She has a background in journalism, finance (Vanguard, Goldman Sachs, State Street, First Trust), and cannabis investing, and is a New York Times best‑selling author.
