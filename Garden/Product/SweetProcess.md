SweetProcess is a cloud-based workflow management software designed to help businesses and organizations streamline their standard operating procedures (SOPs), policies, and checklists [1]. It enables teams to document and optimize their processes efficiently, allowing for seamless task management and collaboration [1]. By using SweetProcess, users can ensure consistency and accuracy in their business operations, making it easier for teams to train new employees and maintain productivity [1].
