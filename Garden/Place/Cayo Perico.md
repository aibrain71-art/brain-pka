Cayo Perico is a fictional private island in the Caribbean, off the coast of Colombia, featured in Grand Theft Auto Online as part of The Cayo Perico Heist update. It is owned by the notorious drug lord Juan "El Rubio" Strickler and is considered one of the most secure private islands in the world.
