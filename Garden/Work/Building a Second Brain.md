Building a Second Brain is a methodology for saving and systematically reminding us of the ideas, inspirations, insights, and connections we've gained through our experience [2, 4]. It is a system for personal knowledge management that exists outside of your own head [5]. It expands our memory and our intellect using the modern tools of technology and networks [4].
