Dr. Georgia Ede is a Harvard-trained psychiatrist specializing in nutritional and metabolic psychiatry. She has over 20 years of experience and focuses on using nutrition-based therapies for mental health conditions. She writes about food and the brain for Psychology Today and her website DiagnosisDiet.
