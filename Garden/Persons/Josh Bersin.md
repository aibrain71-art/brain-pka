Josh Bersin (born 1966) is an American industry analyst, founder of Bersin & Associates (now part of Deloitte), and a leading expert on human resources, talent management, and workplace learning. He writes a popular blog on influence without formal authority, which includes the quoted passage about tasks versus outcomes.
