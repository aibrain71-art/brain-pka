Need to read the experimental design prompt again that was presented at the beginning of this video?

Experimental Design Prompt

You want to determine if cooler or warmer temperatures* have an effect on how long the pupa stage lasts in mealworms. You place 10 mealworms in a cool environment and 10 mealworms in a warm environment. You also have 10 mealworms kept at room temperature (consider this baseline). Once they reach the pupa stage, you plan to record the average number of days they spend in this stage.

*using mealworm safe temperatures
